ABOUT US

About Healing Hands Health Care

Healing Hands Health Care is a registered non-profit organization committed to empowering the poorest and most vulnerable in our society. Founded under the visionary leadership of Mr. Shah Khalid (Founder) and Dr. Asma Asad (General Secretary), we deliver free healthcare, education, rehabilitation, and women’s skill-development initiatives—all with the aim of fostering sustainable growth and dignity for every individual.

Our Journey & Impact

Over the years, Healing Hands has carried out numerous life-changing programs that have touched thousands of lives.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

To provide free medical care, rehabilitation for the differently abled, educational support for underserved children, and livelihood training for women— so that no one is held back by their financial situation.

Vision

A society where health, education, and opportunity are fundamental rights—not privileges—where every community thrives, and every person is respected and empowered.

Empowering communities through healthcare, education, and sustainable support — together, we create lasting change.
